Understanding the Consistency Metrics

To ensure fair rankings, these metrics filter out pace differences between sessions and vehicle classes:

Average Lap

The overall mean of the driver's lap times across all analyzed race sessions.

Within-Session Std Dev

Calculated by pooling variance within each session. This ignores differences in base speeds between different classes (e.g. shifter vs cadet) or track layouts to measure true lap-to-lap variation.

Coefficient of Variation (CV %)

The primary metric for driving consistency, computed as the weighted average of session CVs (Std Dev / Mean). A lower percentage represents more repeatable lap times relative to the driver's pace.
