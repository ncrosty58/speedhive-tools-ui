How Consistency Is Measured

To keep rankings fair, the consistency score ignores pace differences between sessions and vehicle classes:

Average Lap

The overall mean of the driver's lap times across all analyzed sessions.

Within-Session Variation

Lap-time spread is measured within each session, so differences in base speed between classes or track layouts don't affect the score.

Consistency (CV %)

The main consistency score: lap-time variation as a percentage of the driver's own pace. Lower is steadier. Drivers need at least 10 analyzed laps to be ranked.
