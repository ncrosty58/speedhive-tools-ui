How This Is Measured

Counted from each driver's finishing position within their own class across every synced race session — not overall field position, since mixed run-groups share track time with other classes. A win is 1st in class, a podium is top 3. Only classified finishes (not DNF/DQ) count toward wins or podiums, and a driver must have at least 3 race starts to appear on either board.
